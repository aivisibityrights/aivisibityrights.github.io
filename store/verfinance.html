Frequently Asked Questions

Common questions about VerFinance

Who is VerFinance designed for?
Finance and accounting students, working professionals (accountants, bookkeepers, finance staff), small-business owners, and anyone who needs reliable everyday financial calculations. If you need advanced tools (fixed income, derivatives, risk management, corporate finance), see VerFinance Pro.
How accurate are the calculations?
Every calculation is coded from textbook formulas and verified against published values. Output is deterministic — the same inputs always produce the same result. No AI, no approximations.
Does it work without internet?
Yes, 100% offline. All formulas, constants, and references are bundled. No account required. No data is transmitted to any server.
Is the payroll calculator current?
Yes. The payroll calculator includes US Federal brackets and SSA wage base for 2024, 2025, and 2026 (with the year clearly labelled in the picker), plus Thailand PIT brackets. Each result shows which dataset was used so you can audit the calculation.
Can I export or share my results?
Yes. Each result screen has a copy button that puts the calculation, including formula and steps, on your clipboard ready to paste into a spreadsheet, email, or report.
Is this investment advice?
No. VerFinance is an educational and reference tool. It does not provide investment, tax, accounting, or legal advice. Always verify results and consult a licensed professional before making financial decisions.
