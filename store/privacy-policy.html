This Privacy Policy describes how we collect, use, and protect your information when you use our apps.

Summary: Our apps are designed with privacy first. We do NOT collect, store, or transmit any personal data to external servers. All data stays on your device.

1. Information We Collect

1.1 Personal Data - None

We do not collect any personally identifiable information. Our apps do not require:

  • Account registration or login
  • Email address, phone number, or name
  • Location data or GPS access
  • Contacts access

1.2 Camera & Photos

Some apps (such as VerPet, VerRoast, VerVibe) may request access to your camera or photo library. Photos are:

  • Processed on-device or sent to our AI service for analysis
  • Not stored on our servers after processing
  • Used solely to generate app results (e.g., pet thought readings)
  • Never shared with third parties

1.3 Usage Data

Our apps do not currently use any analytics or tracking services. We do not track how you use the app, which features you access, or how often you open the app.

1.4 Data Stored on Device

Our apps store the following data locally on your device only:

  • Birth date/time information you enter for horoscope calculations
  • Calculation inputs and results for engineering/science apps
  • Test/quiz results for personality apps
  • Game scores and progress
  • Reading history and saved profiles
  • App preferences and settings
  • Purchase status (unlocked/free)

This data is stored using your device's local storage (AsyncStorage) and is never transmitted to any server.

2. In-App Purchases

Our apps offer one-time purchases to unlock full features. These purchases are processed entirely by:

  • Apple App Store (iOS) - governed by Apple's Privacy Policy
  • Google Play Store (Android) - governed by Google's Privacy Policy

We use RevenueCat as our purchase management service. RevenueCat may process anonymous purchase data. See RevenueCat's Privacy Policy.

We do not have access to your payment information (credit card, billing address, etc.).

3. Third-Party Services

Our apps currently do not integrate any third-party services for analytics, advertising, or data collection. If this changes in the future, we will update this policy accordingly.

4. Data Sharing

We do not share, sell, rent, or trade any user data with third parties. Since we don't collect data, there is nothing to share.

5. Children's Privacy (COPPA)

Our apps do not collect any personal information from anyone, including children under 13. Our apps are safe for users of all ages. We comply with the Children's Online Privacy Protection Act (COPPA) and similar international regulations.

6. Data Security

Since all data is stored locally on your device:

  • Your data is protected by your device's built-in security (passcode, Face ID, etc.)
  • Uninstalling the app removes all stored data
  • No data breach risk from our side as we don't store data on servers

7. Your Rights (GDPR)

Under GDPR and similar data protection laws, you have the right to:

  • Access - View your data directly in the app (history/settings)

Since we don't collect data on servers, there is no data for us to delete, modify, or provide to you beyond what's already on your device.
