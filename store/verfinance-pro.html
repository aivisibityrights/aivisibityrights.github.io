Why a Calculator App, Not ChatGPT?

✓
Verified formulas, no hallucinations — Black-Scholes, Cox-Ross-Rubinstein, Markowitz, Altman coded directly from the original papers. AI chatbots cannot reliably reproduce these formulas without arithmetic error.

Deterministic output, every time — Same inputs always produce the same result. AI responses drift across sessions. Your valuation cannot depend on sampling temperature.

100% offline, zero data exfiltration — Nothing leaves your device. No cloud, no logging, no exposure of deal terms or portfolio positions to a third-party LLM.

Frequently Asked Questions

Common questions about VerFinance Pro

Which option pricing models does VerFinance Pro support?
VerFinance Pro includes the Black-Scholes-Merton closed-form model for European options (with full Greeks: Delta, Gamma, Theta, Vega, Rho), and the Cox-Ross-Rubinstein binomial tree for both European and American options with multi-step resolution. Put-call parity, futures cost-of-carry, and interest rate swap valuation are also included. All models are coded directly from the original papers.
How accurate are the calculations?
Every calculation is coded directly from the original peer-reviewed paper or CFA / CPA curriculum reference and verified against textbook values. Deterministic output: same inputs always produce the same result. No AI, no approximations — just the formulas.
Does it work without internet?
Yes, 100% offline. All formulas, constants, reference tables, and citations are bundled into the app. Nothing is transmitted to any server. No account, no tracking, no data collection. Open and use it anywhere — trading floor, client meeting, flight, rural locations with no connectivity.
What risk management tools are included?
Value at Risk via three methods (parametric / variance-covariance, historical simulation, Monte Carlo), Conditional VaR (Expected Shortfall), Beta via regression against a market benchmark, Sortino Ratio (downside deviation), Treynor Ratio (systematic risk-adjusted), Information Ratio (active management skill / tracking error), and Maximum Drawdown with recovery analysis.
Who is this app for?
CFA candidates (Levels I, II, III), CPA candidates, financial analysts, investment bankers, private equity associates, portfolio managers, commercial real estate underwriters, corporate treasury professionals, and finance students. The Guide pages explain each calculator in plain language — making the app a learning tool as well as a desk reference.
Can I use it for CFA or CPA exam preparation?
Yes. The formulas and structure mirror the CFA Institute curriculum (Levels I — III) and CPA financial accounting / advanced financial accounting topics. Every formula is cited, and the Guide pages include key terms and worked examples appropriate for exam prep. Use it to work practice problems offline.
What about LBO modeling and private equity tools?
The Corporate Finance category includes an LBO model with entry / exit equity, debt schedule with annual repayment, MOIC (Multiple on Invested Capital) with time adjustment, EV / EBITDA multiples, comparable trading analysis, two-stage FCFF and FCFE, Adjusted Present Value (unlevered NPV plus tax shield), and Economic Value Added. Full underwriting support for small and mid-market deals.
Is this investment advice?
No. VerFinance Pro is a professional reference and educational calculator. It does not provide investment, tax, accounting, or legal advice. All output must be verified independently and interpreted by a qualified professional in the full context of the specific transaction, client, or portfolio. Past performance of any model does not guarantee future results.
